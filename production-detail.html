Production Details

Welcome to Waterale, where we believe that clean and pure water is a fundamental right for everyone. We take immense pride in our 13-stage water filtration machine and purification process, ensuring that you receive the highest quality water for your well-being.

Our advanced filtration system utilizes cutting-edge technology to remove impurities and contaminants, leaving you with water that is both refreshing and healthy.

Let's take a closer look at our 13-stage purification process:

1. Sediment Pre-Filter: This initial stage eliminates larger particles such as sand, sediment, and rust from the water.

2. Granular Activated Carbon: The water then passes through a layer of activated carbon, which effectively removes chlorine, chemicals, and unpleasant odors.

3. Carbon Block Filter: Here, the water is further purified as it passes through a densely packed carbon block, reducing impurities, heavy metals, and volatile organic compounds (VOCs).

4. Reverse Osmosis (RO) Membrane: This crucial stage uses a semipermeable membrane to remove dissolved solids, including bacteria, viruses, lead, arsenic, fluoride, and other harmful contaminants.

5. Post Carbon Filter: After the RO membrane, the water is refined once again to eliminate any remaining taste or odor.

6. Alkaline pH Filter: In this stage, the water is infused with beneficial minerals to increase alkalinity and provide you with a refreshing, balanced pH level.

7. Mineralization Filter: Essential minerals such as calcium, magnesium, and potassium are reintroduced into the water, promoting your overall health and well-being.

8. Far-Infrared Filter: This filter utilizes far-infrared technology to enhance the water's bioavailability and aid in better hydration.

9. Ultraviolet (UV) Sterilization: To ensure complete disinfection, the water is exposed to UV light, effectively neutralizing any remaining bacteria, viruses, or other microorganisms.

10. Ozonation: Ozone is introduced to the water to destroy any remaining bacteria, viruses, or other organic impurities, guaranteeing its purity.

11. Activated Carbon Filter: This additional layer of activated carbon further enhances the water's taste, removing any residual odors or flavors.

12. Final Polishing Filter: The water undergoes a final filtration process to ensure absolute clarity and purity before it reaches your glass.

13. UV Final Sterilization: To provide an additional layer of safety, the water passes through a UV sterilization chamber once more, eliminating any potential contaminants.
